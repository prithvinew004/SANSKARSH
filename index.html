About Us

SANSKARSH 2.0: Electrifying the Vegas Spirit Welcome to the grand spectacle of SANSKARSH 2.0, where the 40th batch proudly presents an electrifying extravaganza that captures the pulsating energy of Las Vegas! Get ready to roll the dice and hit the jackpot as we bring you a fusion of technology, entertainment, and innovation like never before. Step into the glitz and glamour of Sin City right here at our campus as we transform it into a dazzling casino wonderland. From the iconic neon lights to the vibrant streets, every corner will resonate with the spirit of Las Vegas, promising an unforgettable experience. Prepare to be awestruck as our electrical branch takes center stage, showcasing cutting-edge advancements and groundbreaking inventions that push the boundaries of innovation. Witness the brilliance of our students as they unveil their projects, demonstrating their mastery over circuits, automation, and renewable energy solutions. But that's not all! SANSKARSH 2.0 is not just about technology; it's a celebration of creativity and talent. Immerse yourself in a whirlwind of activities, from electrifying performances by talented artists to thrilling casino-style games that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Indulge your senses with a culinary journey that mirrors the diverse flavors of Las Vegas, offering a tantalizing array of cuisines to satisfy every palate. And don't forget to capture the magic of the moment at our photo booths, where you can immortalize memories with friends and classmates. As the night unfolds, feel the adrenaline rush as you take part in our exhilarating competitions and challenges, testing your skills and knowledge against the best and brightest minds in the field of electrical engineering. So mark your calendars and join us for an unforgettable evening of excitement, innovation, and sheer Vegas-style fun at SANSKARSH 2.0. Let's ignite the spark of inspiration and embark on a journey that promises to illuminate the future of technology. See you at the tables!
